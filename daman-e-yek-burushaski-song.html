Daman E Yek: A Historic Burushaski Musical Experience

A profound song celebrating the abundant beauty and order in nature, filled with symbols of faith, hope, and healing.

This song is a simple yet profound appreciation for the abundant beauty and order in nature. Filled with symbols and metaphors, it expresses gratitude to the guardian of life for the many blessings surrounding us. Numerous examples from nature are linked with faith, hope, and the balance of pain and healing.

Complete Lyrics & Translation

The following presents the complete Burushaski lyrics, their transliteration, and the official English translation in sequence.

Verse 1

Burushaski Script

کیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

ہیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

Transliteration

ken damane yek yaṭe

hen damane yek yaṭe

English Translation

Starting with the name of God

With his name who is only one

Verse 2

Burushaski Script

تھُوا برِݩ ہَکِم ہَکِن

شُوا برِݩ ہَکِم ہَکِن

تَوَہ نیࣷتے ین گُریࣷنچی

پِن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

Transliteration

thuwa bariṅ hakim hakin

śuwa bariṅ hakim hakin

tawah nete yan gurenći

pin damane yek yaṭe

English Translation

Learn to learn the new knowledge/information

Learn to learn the right knowledge

Gracefully pick it up with your hand

That pen with the name of God

Verse 3

Burushaski Script

گُماھڵچُم متھن نیࣷتے

گُیا دُنیࣷش ڎھن نیࣷتے

سیࣷسےࣷ اُݩگی گوسُم برِݩ

سیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

Transliteration

gumahlćum mathan nete

guya duneś chan nete

sese uṅgi gosum bariṅ

sen damane yek yaṭe

English Translation

Put away your fear

Clear your head of all the thoughts

The things that your heart desires to say

Say it out loud in the name of God

Verse 4

Burushaski Script

اہیࣷراسےࣷ اُوُو جُوا

ہئیࣷ څھیࣷنےࣷ ممُو جُوا

سردارےࣷ کا پھیࣷتِݩ منی

تیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

Transliteration

aherase uuwu juwa

hai c̣hene mamu juwa

sardare ka pheṭiṅ mani

ṭen damane yek yaṭe

English Translation

When you pass that weird human

The ones armed with weapons

Raise your voice to make it fly

The bird with the name of God

Cultural & Linguistic Significance

This project goes beyond music; it is an act of cultural preservation. By producing high-quality artistic content in Burushaski—a language with limited digital resources—DigiCell International contributes to keeping the language vibrant and accessible for a global audience. The inclusion of meticulous transliteration and professional English translation makes this piece of heritage discoverable and meaningful to both native speakers and international enthusiasts.

The collaboration features notable contributors including Sardar Ali Khamosh, a computer science researcher specializing in NLP and low-resource language technology, and Muniza Hashim, whose translation work bridges cultural understanding for global audiences.
