Daman E Yek: A Historic Burushaski Musical Experience

A profound song celebrating the abundant beauty and order in nature, filled with symbols of faith, hope, and healing.

This song is a simple yet profound appreciation for the abundant beauty and order in nature. Filled with symbols and metaphors, it expresses gratitude to the guardian of life for the many blessings surrounding us. Numerous examples from nature are linked with faith, hope, and the balance of pain and healing.

Lyrics & Translation

The following presents the Burushaski lyrics, their transliteration, and the official English translation.

کیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ
ہیࣷن دمنےࣷ ییࣷک یٹےࣷ

ken damane yek yaṭe
hen damane yek yaṭe

English Subtitles/Translation 1:
Starting with the name of God
With his name who is only one

Cultural & Linguistic Significance

This project goes beyond music; it is an act of cultural preservation. By producing high-quality artistic content in Burushaski—a language with limited digital resources—DigiCell International contributes to keeping the language vibrant and accessible for a global audience[citation:3][citation:8]. The inclusion of meticulous transliteration and professional English translation makes this piece of heritage discoverable and meaningful to both native speakers and international enthusiasts[citation:6].
